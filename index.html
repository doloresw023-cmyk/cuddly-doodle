Bedrock Add-on (Cosmetic Skeleton)

A harmless add-on skeleton you can install in Minecraft Bedrock. It contains manifest files and instructions—add your textures/behavior files to customize.

  • Location in repo: bedrock-addon/
  • To install: create a .mcpack by zipping the folder, then change extension to .mcpack and open with Minecraft on Bedrock.
