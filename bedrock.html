Bedrock Add-on (Cosmetic Skeleton)

This folder contains a minimal `manifest.json`. Add textures, models, or behavior files to customize. Packaging instructions are below.

Files

  • bedrock-addon/manifest.json
  • bedrock-addon/README.md

Packaging (.mcpack)

To create a .mcpack you can open in Minecraft Bedrock:

zip -r addon.zip *
mv addon.zip my-addon.mcpack
open my-addon.mcpack on your Bedrock device

Note: Update UUIDs in `manifest.json` to unique values before publishing.
